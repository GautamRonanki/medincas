Our Story

The name Medincas is a play on "Med" and "Incas." Incas derived from the Inca civilization which was well-known for a range of feats, such as creating one of the largest empires in history and developing impressive agricultural techniques. Towards it, Medincas developed its own processes for producing captivating visuals for the LifeSciences industry. Medincas has a strong clientele with its unique content, science and art, making it the perfect platform for any Pharma company.

Our work revolves around medical animation, and we use our scientific storytelling and drug visualization to help develop a pharmaceutical marketing campaign. From huge pharmaceutical giants to small innovative biotechnology companies (and everyone in between), we have created work for a wide range of different companies.

Our goal is to create outstanding and effective visual communication digital design, including high-end medical animation, illustration, and interactive content that engages and elevates medical science, platform technology, and brand.
